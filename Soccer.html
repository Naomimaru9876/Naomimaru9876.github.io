What is Soccer?

Soccer, also known as football in many parts of the world, is the most popular sport globally. It's played by two teams of eleven players who try to score goals by getting the ball into the opposing team's net.

Basic Rules

  • The game lasts 90 minutes (two halves of 45 minutes).
  • Only goalkeepers can use their hands inside the penalty area.
  • Key rules include offside, fouls, yellow/red cards, and penalty kicks.

The Appeal of Soccer

Soccer is more than just a game — it's a global language. From local pitches to world-class stadiums, it brings people together through skill, emotion, and incredible moments. The simplicity of the rules and the beauty of the game make it easy for anyone to fall in love with.
